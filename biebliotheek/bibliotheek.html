Daily Reccomendations

The Long Way to a Small, Angry Planet by Becky Chambers is a character-driven science fiction novel that follows the diverse crew of the spaceship Wayfarer as they embark on a journey through space. Exploring themes of identity, belonging, and the complexities of interspecies relationships, it blends optimism with deep emotional storytelling.

The Three-Body Problem by Liu Cixin is a hard science fiction novel set during China’s Cultural Revolution and beyond. It explores humanity’s first contact with an alien civilization, delving into physics, political intrigue, and existential threats. The story examines the implications of advanced technology and cosmic-scale conflict.
